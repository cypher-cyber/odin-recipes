Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • 1 clove of garlic (optional)
  • Olive oil (optional)
  • 1/2 cup of pasta water (reserved)

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook pancetta until crispy. If using garlic, add it to the pan for flavor.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, pecorino, parmesan, and black pepper.
  4. Once spaghetti is cooked, reserve some pasta water and drain the rest.
  5. Add hot spaghetti to the pancetta pan and remove from heat.
  6. Quickly mix in the egg mixture, adding reserved pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with parsley and extra cheese if desired.
