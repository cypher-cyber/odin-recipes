chicken soup

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 2 celery stalks
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 6 cups of water
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 cup of noodles (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large pot, combine the chicken, carrots, onion, celery, garlic, bay leaf, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add water and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for about 1 hour or until the chicken is cooked through.
  4. Remove the chicken from the pot and let it cool.
  5. Shred the chicken and return it to the pot.
  6. If using noodles, add them to the pot and cook according to package instructions.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.
